It's now up to you

Find the issue that you're passionate about.

Help keep the world linked, because together we can change anything.

Websites are like stories. They usually end. They have a beginning, a middle, an end, always in that order. But stories aren't always that simple. Life is almost never that simple. Complexity requires us to re-think the way we are. If we don't continue to challenge, to imagine, to think about why we act a certain way or why something is the way it is, we risk a single story. We risk a patriarchal world, a world where not everyone feels or is equal. I want to help stop that, and I hope you do too. In my case, I can help prevent this digitally. But maybe you, with your own sets of talents and skills, can find ways to incorporate solutions in your interests.

By merging our different areas into one and educating each other, we can undo the patriachy as a whole. But it takes all of us combined.
